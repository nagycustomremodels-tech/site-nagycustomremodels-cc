Full-Service Remodeling in Jacksonville

Every Trade.
One Trusted Team.

From a single room refresh to a whole-home transformation, Nagy Custom Remodeling handles every phase in-house. No juggling subcontractors. No finger-pointing. Just one team accountable for every detail.

Most remodeling projects touch multiple trades — framing, tile, paint, trim. Hiring separate contractors for each one creates gaps, delays, and blame games. Nagy handles it all under one roof. One point of contact, one standard of quality, one crew that owns the outcome from start to finish.

01

Kitchen Remodeling

Where your home's value lives.

The kitchen sells the house — and it's where your family gathers every day. We handle full kitchen remodels across Jacksonville: custom cabinetry, countertop installation, backsplash tile, flooring, lighting, and plumbing coordination. You get a space that works harder and looks sharper than anything off a showroom floor.

02

Bathroom Remodeling

Your daily reset, redesigned.

A dated bathroom drags down your entire home. We build bathrooms that feel intentional — walk-in showers with clean tile work, floating vanities, proper waterproofing, and fixtures that last. From guest baths to primary suites, Jacksonville homeowners trust us to get the wet work right.

03

Flooring

The foundation of every great room.

Hardwood, luxury vinyl plank (LVP), laminate, and porcelain tile — we install it all with precision and proper subfloor prep. Florida humidity demands materials and methods that won't buckle or gap in two years. We select and install flooring built for Northeast Florida's climate, so your investment holds up.

04

Tile Work

Precision you can see. Durability you can feel.

Tile is only as good as the hands that set it. Our crew specializes in shower tile installation, kitchen and bathroom backsplash installation, floor patterns, and accent walls across Jacksonville homes. Straight lines, consistent spacing, waterproof membranes where it matters. The details that separate professional tile from a weekend project.

05

Painting

Inside out — clean lines, rich color, zero shortcuts.

Interior and exterior painting done right. We prep every surface before a brush touches the wall — patching, sanding, priming, and taping with care. Our painters deliver crisp edges, even coats, and zero drips. Whether it's an accent wall, a full interior, or the complete exterior of your home, we leave it looking magazine-ready.

06

Interior Finish Work

Seamless walls. Flawless trim. Every detail done right.

From drywall installation and repair to the finishing details that make a room feel complete. We handle drywall hanging, taping, and Level 5 finish — plus baseboards and trim, door installation and replacement, window installation, cabinet installation, and countertop installation. One crew, every interior trade.

07

Exterior Work

Curb appeal and protection from the outside in.

Everything your home's exterior needs under one crew. We build and repair decks, install and repair siding, put up fences, and handle roof repairs — even minor ones before they become major problems. Jacksonville weather demands exteriors that are built right, and we bring the same standard to every job outside your walls.

08

Whole-Home Renovation

One vision. One team. Every room.

When it's time to gut it and start over, you need a crew that can manage the full scope without losing momentum. We coordinate demolition, structural changes, electrical, plumbing rough-ins, and every finish trade — delivering a complete transformation on schedule. One project manager. One standard. Every room done right.
