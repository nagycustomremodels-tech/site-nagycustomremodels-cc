Common Questions

FAQ

How long does a kitchen remodel take?

Most kitchen remodels in Jacksonville take between 4 and 8 weeks depending on the scope — a cosmetic refresh runs shorter, while a full gut with layout changes and new plumbing runs longer. We provide a detailed timeline during your estimate so you know exactly what to expect before work begins.

Do you provide free estimates?

Yes — always. We'll schedule a walkthrough of your space, discuss your goals and budget, and deliver a written estimate with line-item pricing. There's no fee and no obligation. We believe the estimate is where trust starts, and we take it seriously.

Are you licensed and insured in Florida?

Absolutely. Nagy Custom Remodeling is fully licensed and insured to operate in the state of Florida. We carry general liability and workers' compensation coverage. We're happy to provide documentation before your project starts — and we encourage you to ask every contractor you consider for the same.

Do you handle permits and inspections?

Yes. We pull all required permits with the City of Jacksonville and coordinate every inspection. Permit work protects your investment and your home's resale value. We handle the paperwork and scheduling so you don't have to navigate the process yourself.

What areas do you serve?

We serve all of Jacksonville and Northeast Florida — including the Beaches, Ponte Vedra, Mandarin, San Marco, Riverside, Avondale, Ortega, Arlington, Southside, and St. Johns County. If you're within the greater Jacksonville metro, we'd love to hear about your project.
