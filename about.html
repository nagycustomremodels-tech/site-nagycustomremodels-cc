Who We Are

From Passion to Every Last Detail

Nagy Custom Remodeling started with a simple belief: homeowners deserve a contractor who treats their house like his own. Founded in Jacksonville, we built this company one honest project at a time — showing up early, communicating clearly, and never cutting corners. That reputation didn't come from marketing. It came from the work itself.

Craftsmanship isn't a buzzword here. It's the reason we measure twice, obsess over grout lines, and stay late to get the trim right. Every kitchen, every bathroom, every floor we install carries our name. We take that personally. From demolition to the final walk-through, precision drives everything we do.

Jacksonville is home. We know the neighborhoods, the building codes, the humidity that punishes cheap materials. We also know this is a diverse city. Our bilingual team speaks fluent English and Portuguese, so every homeowner we work with gets clear communication from estimate to completion — no confusion, no surprises.
